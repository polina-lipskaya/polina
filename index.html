Improving Financial Transparency Through an Invoice Dashboard

Challenge. Leadership lacked a single source of truth for vendor invoices across multiple lines of business, limiting visibility into variance, call costs, and bonus/training fees.

Approach. Built an Excel dashboard consolidating two vendors with monthly billed amounts, bonuses, training, budget vs. actual variance, and call volume integration for cost-per-call.

Impact. Delivered clear monthly snapshots, reduced reconciliation time, enabled data-driven vendor decisions, and created a scalable framework.

Driving Vendor Excellence with a Customer Service Transformation Survey

Challenge. No structured, repeatable way to evaluate the Customer Service Transformation program across vendors and stakeholders.

Approach. Designed a biannual survey for vendor ops managers, account teams, directors, and quality/training/workforce; mixed Likert + open-ended items; synthesized in executive dashboards.

Impact. Established a feedback loop, surfaced quantitative/qualitative insights, and highlighted strengths and improvement opportunities.

Comparative Analysis of Metabolomic Profiles in Fit vs. Frail Elderly

Challenge. Explore whether peripheral serum metabolomics with ML can provide objective frailty prediction beyond subjective clinical scales.

Approach. Python pipeline using XGBoost (GPU), Optuna tuning, and SHAP for interpretability; excluded clinical frailty scale to focus on metabolomic + demographic signal.

Impact. Strong classification performance; identified top metabolites associated with frailty; delivered interpretable SHAP insights for healthcare stakeholders.
